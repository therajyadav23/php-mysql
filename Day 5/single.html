About This Project

This page demonstrates how to create a clean parallax scrolling effect using Bootstrap 3 and a bit of CSS. It's great for learning how to build visually interesting one-page websites.

The parallax effect happens when the background image stays fixed while the foreground content scrolls — giving a nice sense of depth to your website design.

Places

Manali is a high-altitude Himalayan resort town in India’s northern Himachal Pradesh state. It has a reputation as a backpacking center and honeymoon destination.

Ladakh is both the largest and the second least populous union territory of India.

Kedarnath refers to a sacred pilgrimage site and a mountain peak in the Garhwal Himalayas of Uttarakhand, India. The Kedarnath Temple, dedicated to Lord Shiva, is a major destination for Hindu pilgrims as it is one of the 12 Jyotirlingas and part of the Char Dham circuit. The Kedarnath mountain peak is a 6,940-meter-high, challenging climb for experienced mountaineers.
